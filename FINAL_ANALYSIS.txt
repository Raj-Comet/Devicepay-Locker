### REQUIREMENT 5: Auto-Restore After Factory Reset
**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE & VERIFIED

**Evidence:**

- ✅ `app/src/main/kotlin/com/emishield/locker/service/EmiCheckWorker.kt`
- ✅ WorkManager integration for persistence

**Flow:**
```
1. Device boots → BOOT_COMPLETED broadcast triggered
2. BootReceiver catches event
3. Starts EmiCheckService
4. Schedules WorkManager PeriodicWorkRequest
5. Reapplies device policies
6. Resumes EMI monitoring
```

**Features:**
```
✅ Automatic boot detection
✅ Service auto-start
✅ WorkManager persistence (survives app kill)
✅ Policy re-application
✅ 15-minute monitoring schedule
```
